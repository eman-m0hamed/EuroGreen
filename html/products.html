Biopesticide

EuroGreen Technology is leading a European revolution with innovative natural chelated-fertilizers, serving as soil fertilizers, hydrogels, and growth promoters. These advanced formulas offer enhanced disease protection, supporting versatile expansion in vertical and horizontal farming. Our biocompatible, biodegradable formulas feature biopolymer derivatives with carefully engineered chemical modifications. Packed with essential plant elements, our water-based formulas are customized for organic agriculture, prioritizing environmental friendliness for both ecosystems and our discerning customers.
