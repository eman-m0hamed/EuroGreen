About Us

Who Are We?

EuroGreen Technology is a new born innovation startup established through the Italian startup hub program

as an innovative enterprise. In June 2023, EuroGreen Technology has been officially registered in Italian chamber of commerce.

Founder and CEO - Rania Morsi, PhD

PhD in science with 11 years of post-doctorate experience in research and development. Holder of 2 patents, 42 research articles published in Q1 journals, 9 contributions in scientific book chapters and review articles. Supervised numerous master and doctorate theses.

Currently, she has an association research position in the Italian national research council beside a permanent position in Egyptian research institute as a full-professor. She has been contributed as a member, co-PI and PI in many national and international collaboration projects; lastly, she has awarded a Horizon 2020 Marie Curie individual followship. Marie Curie followship was a unique opportunity and a turning point in her carrier.

Marie Curie Alumni association meeting in Vienna 2019 was inspiring in terms of numerous examples of researchers entrepreneurs’ startups. More inspiration was during sharing in AquaTech International water week, RAI Amsterdam, Netherlands 2019. she shared a lecture at Meet the Expert stage of the Innovation Lab in addition, she had the chance to see the hundreds of Start-ups and SMEs in the related topics.
