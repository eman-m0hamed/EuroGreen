Who Are We?

EuroGreen Technology is a new born innovation startup established through the Italian startup hub program

as an innovative enterprise. In June 2023, EuroGreen Technology has been officially registered in Italian chamber of commerce.

Founder and CEO - Rania Morsi, PhD

Dr. Rania Morsy, a seasoned professional in science, holds a Ph.D. with 11 years of post-doctorate experience in research and development. Notable achievements include 2 patents, 42 research articles in Q1 journals, and contributions to 9 scientific book chapters and review articles. She has supervised numerous theses, showcasing her commitment to fostering the next generation of researchers.

Currently, Dr. Morsy holds a research position at the Italian National Research Council and is a full professor at an Egyptian research institute. Engaging in various capacities, she has been involved in numerous national and international collaborative projects. She was recently awarded a Horizon 2020 Marie Curie individual fellowship, a pivotal moment in her career. Dr. Rania Morsy continues to be a driving force in advancing science, research, and innovation.
