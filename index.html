Startup Activities

In the startup's early stage, the main activity is the production of biomaterials for agriculture applications. The project introduces new techniques for producing chitosan-based formulations with various applications.

biomaterials production for agriculture applications is the main activity. The project reveals new techniques for producing new chitosan-based formulations that are completely water soluble, biocompatible and biodegradable for agricultural and food processing purposes. Growing number of food processing industries in Europe has led to a large import of chitosan mainly from Asia pacific regions. In addition, recently, the European Parliament Regulation approved using chitosan derivative in food industry which is expected to further increase the demands on chitosan and its derivatives. European production of such products to satisfy the European market demand is thus a strategic Autonomy. The project is introducing a climate neutral economy which fulfils the instructions listed in climate targets set in Paris Agreement and the EU Green Deal and Fit for 55 (Green transition).

The formula has been primary evaluated on small scale using planting pots and then large-scale field trials were performed. In Egypt. Alsharqia, the proposed formula was used for seedlings treatment to avoid roots rot and then another formula was added to irrigation water. In opposite to the untreated, onion treated with the produced formula shows a continuous growing with healthy green leaves with strong dense roots and the yield is higher. Another field trial was in the Delta region in Egypt on green peppers under harsh conditions using stunted seedlings with no apex growing. Unexpectedly during the first two weeks, the growing apex begins to apper and the plants look much healthier.
