About Us

EuroGreen Technology, an emerging innovative startup, was founded under the Italian startup hub program (insert hyperlink: https://italiastartuphub.mise.gov.it/) as a pioneering enterprise. In June 2023, it was formally registered with the Italian Chamber of Commerce.

Vision

To harness the power of nature and innovations in science and technology to pioneer eco-friendly sustainable solutions for high-performance practices.

EuroGreen Technology envisions pioneering sustainable solutions, transforming the agricultural biostimulant sector in alignment with Farm-to-Fork strategies and UN SDGs. Our vision extends to leading advancements in various sectors, promoting climate change mitigation, ensuring food security, and fostering fair economic returns. Committed to sustainable practices and innovation, our vision is rooted in the green circular economy, utilizing waste management to reduce chemical risks and prioritizing customer and environmental considerations in technological innovations.

Mision

To apply cutting-edge technology and scientific findings to design and develop innovative biomaterials that support sustainable and resilient agriculture.

EuroGreen Technology is on a mission to be a key contributor to sustainable agriculture, introducing new-to-market biostimulant solutions. Grounded in professionalism and sustainability, our commitment is to revolutionize biostimulant products across diverse sectors, satisfying European market demands for organic agriculture transformation and contributing to the green transition with a set of biomaterials.
