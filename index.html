Startup Activities

In the startup's early stage, the main activity is the production of biomaterials for agriculture applications. The project introduces new techniques for producing chitosan-based formulations with various applications.

biomaterials production for agriculture applications is the main activity. The project reveals new techniques for producing new chitosan-based formulations that are completely water soluble, biocompatible and biodegradable for agricultural and food processing purposes. Growing number of food processing industries in Europe has led to a large import of chitosan mainly from Asia pacific regions. In addition, recently, the European Parliament Regulation approved using chitosan derivative in food industry which is expected to further increase the demands on chitosan and its derivatives. European production of such products to satisfy the European market demand is thus a strategic Autonomy. The project is introducing a climate neutral economy which fulfils the instructions listed in climate targets set in Paris Agreement and the EU Green Deal and Fit for 55 (Green transition).

The formula has been primary evaluated on small scale using planting pots and then large-scale field trials were performed. In Egypt. Alsharqia, the proposed formula was used for seedlings treatment to avoid roots rot and then another formula was added to irrigation water. In opposite to the untreated, onion treated with the produced formula shows a continuous growing with healthy green leaves with strong dense roots and the yield is higher. Another field trial was in the Delta region in Egypt on green peppers under harsh conditions using stunted seedlings with no apex growing. Unexpectedly during the first two weeks, the growing apex begins to apper and the plants look much healthier.

Research and development

The Environment

Green circular economy and zero liquid discharge processes beside raising awareness about organic agriculture are extremely important to the environment. Unique characteristic of the proposed formula is naturally improving the soil fertility and the biological balance. The proposed growth promotors can safely increase the crop yield by around 30% and the proposed soil hydrogel can facilitate cultivating crops in dry lands.

The biocompatibility and biodegradability natures of the products grantee a complete degradation and absorption of all the ingredients in the soil without any toxic residues or emissions. This is in line with the European Green Deal; European Fit for 55 , adopted by the commission in July 2021

to reduce net greenhouse gas emissions by at least 55% by 2030 to reach climate neutrality by 2050. This is also in line with the EIC challenge to preserve the European strategic autonomy and reduce the EU dependency on external providers of organic agriculture supplies.

Products

BioMaterials soil Hydrogel Growth promoters soil fertilizers Biomedical Products

EuroGreen Technology is pioneering new European generation of natural chelated-fertilizers with vital added features working simultaneously as soil fertilizer, hydrogel and growth promotor with added protection against diseases which admit the flexible vertical and horizontal farming extension

The formulas are biocompatible and biodegradable, consisting of structurally-designed biopolymer derivatives subjected to a series of chemically engineered modifications. The formula loaded with essential plant's elements and nutrients in a water-base formula for organic agricultural purposes with added acknowledged advantages to the environment and the customers.
